1. Introduction

Welcome to Tujje Safaris. We are a trusted East African tour and travel company offering wildlife safaris, cultural tours, hiking adventures, and lodge bookings.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website or services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:
  • Full name and identification details
  • Email address and phone number
  • Nationality and country of residence
  • Safari booking details and travel dates
  • Passport information (for booking and permits)
  • Payment information (processed securely)
  • Messages sent through contact forms or WhatsApp
  • Device and browser information
  • Location data (if permitted)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:
  • Process safari bookings and reservations
  • Communicate travel updates and confirmations
  • Customize safari itineraries
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send promotional offers (if you agree)
  • Ensure safety and fraud prevention

4. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to:
  • Improve user experience
  • Remember preferences
  • Analyze website traffic
You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share your data with:
  • Hotels and lodges for accommodation booking
  • National park authorities for permits
  • Transport providers (safari vehicles, drivers)
  • Payment processors for secure transactions
  • Government authorities if required by law

6. Data Security

We use modern security measures including encryption, secure servers, and restricted access systems to protect your data.

However, no online system is 100% secure, so we also encourage users to be careful when sharing personal information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing messages
  • Request a copy of your data

8. International Users

Since Tujje Safaris serves international travelers, your data may be processed in Uganda and other East African countries where our partners operate. We ensure your data is handled safely regardless of location.

9. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as:
  • Payment gateways
  • Booking platforms
  • Google Maps and analytics tools
These services have their own privacy policies.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
