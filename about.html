Who We Are

Welcome to Tujje Safaris

Tujje Safaris is a proudly Ugandan tour and travel company dedicated to creating unforgettable journeys across Uganda and East Africa. Whether you dream of tracking mountain gorillas, witnessing the Big Five, exploring breathtaking waterfalls, or experiencing vibrant local cultures, we make every adventure extraordinary.

Our experienced team believes that every traveler deserves a personalized experience. We carefully design each safari to match your interests, schedule, and budget while ensuring comfort, safety, and unforgettable memories.

From airport transfers to luxury safaris, family holidays, honeymoon packages, cultural tours, and wildlife expeditions, Tujje Safaris is your trusted travel companion.
